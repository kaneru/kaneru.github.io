The Asia Escapade — my very first photo zine!

96 pages. 86 street photos. A4 format. Soft cover.

Shot across Malaysia, Brunei, Indonesia, Hong Kong, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Thailand, and Japan. This is a personal collection from my travels around South East and East Asia — an escapade that transformed my life.

This zine includes candid, sometimes gritty pictures of passersby—people I don’t know and will likely never see again. While traveling, I usually immerse myself in the rawness of the streets, approaching it with curiosity and pushing myself to walk further and further.
